Topological antichiral surface states in a magnetic Weyl photonic crystal

Unique properties of antichiral surface states have been observed directly, including the antichiral robust propagation, tilted surface dispersion, a single open Fermi arc connecting two projected WPs.

Observation of Dirac Hierarchy in Three-Dimensional Acoustic Topological Insulators

Acoustic measurements reveal the full hierarchy of Dirac cones and the corresponding hierarchy of band topology in 3D acoustic crystals.
